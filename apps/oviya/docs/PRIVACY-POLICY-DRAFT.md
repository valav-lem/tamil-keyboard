# Yazhi Privacy Policy v1.0 (Draft)

> *"Your data is yours. We are just the custodians."*

## 1. The Promise
**We do not sell your data.** Not to advertisers. Not to governments (without a warrant). Not to anyone.

## 2. What We Collect
- **Nothing unnecessary.**
- **Keyboard:** Keystrokes stay on your device. We only collect crash logs (opt-in).
- **Yazh Social:** Your posts (public), your votes (anonymous). No real names required.
- **Auth:** Phone number (hashed) for login. That's it.

## 3. Where It Lives
- Your data lives on **Indian servers**.
- We comply with the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP)**.

## 4. Your Rights
1.  **Right to Access:** Download everything we have on you.
2.  **Right to Delete:** One button deletes everything. Forever.
3.  **Right to Correct:** Fix any errors instantly.

## 5. Third Parties
- We use **open-source** tools.
- We do not use Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel.
- We use **self-hosted** analytics (Plausible/Matomo).

## 6. Children's Privacy
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.
- Yazh Social is 13+ only.
